Easy Overnight Oats

Start your morning right with these creamy, no-cook overnight oats! Prep them before bed and grab breakfast on your way out the door.

Time needed: 5 minutes prep + 4 hours (or overnight) chilling
Serves 1

Ingredients:
• 1/2 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
• 1/2 cup milk of choice (dairy, almond, oat, etc.)
• 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
• 1 tablespoon chia seeds
• 1 tablespoon maple syrup or honey
• 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
• Pinch of salt

Optional toppings:
• Fresh berries
• Sliced banana
• Nut butter
• Chopped nuts
• Shredded coconut
• Chocolate chips

Steps:
1. Add oats, milk, yogurt, chia seeds, maple syrup, vanilla, and salt to a mason jar or container with a lid.
2. Stir everything together until well combined.
3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight (up to 5 days).
4. In the morning, give it a stir. Add more milk if you prefer a thinner consistency.
5. Top with your favorite toppings and enjoy cold, or microwave for 1-2 minutes if you prefer warm oats.

Pro tip: Make a batch of 5 jars on Sunday for easy breakfasts all week long!

Nutrition info per serving (without toppings):
Calories: 290
Protein: 12g
Carbs: 42g
Fat: 8g
Fiber: 6g
