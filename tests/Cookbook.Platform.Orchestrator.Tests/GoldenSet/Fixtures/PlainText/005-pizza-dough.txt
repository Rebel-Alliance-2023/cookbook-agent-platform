Simple Homemade Pizza Dough

There's nothing quite like homemade pizza! This easy dough recipe makes a crispy yet chewy crust that's perfect every time.

Makes: 2 medium pizzas (12 inch)
Active time: 15 minutes
Rising time: 1-2 hours

INGREDIENTS

3 1/4 cups bread flour (or all-purpose flour)
2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast (one packet)
1 teaspoon sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup warm water (110°F)
2 tablespoons olive oil, plus more for the bowl

DIRECTIONS

1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together flour, yeast, sugar, and salt.

2. Make a well in the center. Pour in warm water and olive oil.

3. Stir with a wooden spoon until a shaggy dough forms.

4. Turn out onto a lightly floured surface. Knead for 8-10 minutes until dough is smooth and springs back when poked.

5. Shape into a ball. Place in a lightly oiled bowl, turning to coat. Cover with plastic wrap or a damp towel.

6. Let rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours until doubled in size.

7. Punch down dough. Divide in half if making two pizzas.

8. On a floured surface, stretch and roll each portion into your desired shape and thickness.

9. Transfer to a pizza peel or baking sheet dusted with cornmeal. Add toppings.

10. Bake at 475°F (245°C) for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.

VARIATIONS:

Whole Wheat: Replace half the bread flour with whole wheat flour
Garlic Herb: Add 1 tbsp dried Italian herbs and 2 minced garlic cloves to dough
Thin Crust: Roll dough very thin and bake on preheated pizza stone

STORAGE: Dough can be refrigerated for up to 3 days or frozen for 3 months. Bring to room temperature before using.
